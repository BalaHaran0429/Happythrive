Builders

What does a builder do?

   Builders build homes, as well as commercial and industrial buildings. They also do renovations, excavations and demolitions. More specifically, builders do particular tasks in construction, including manual labour and operating machinery.

Interior Design

What do you do as an interior designer?

    Interior designers make indoor spaces functional, safe, and beautiful by determining space requirements and selecting essential and decorative items, such as colors, lighting, and materials. They must be able to draw, read, and edit blueprints.

Furnitures

Who makes the furniture?

   A carpenter is a person who makes things out of wood. You could hire a carpenter to build you a dining room table and two long benches. Carpenters specialize in woodworking, making furniture from wood and repairing various wooden things.
